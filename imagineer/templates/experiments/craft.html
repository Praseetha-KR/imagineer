Paper rolls

Magazines and news papers are not waste anymore! Spend about half an hour to transform them to useful products. Here is two examples:


1. Photo frame

Step 1: All you need is - some old magazines/newspapers, scissors, glue & cardboard paper.

Step 2: Take a rectangular piece of cardboard, bend it in the middle, cut a concentric piece of rectangle of one side (see above fig.)

Step 3: Roll papers and glue it, so that it look like straw.

Step 4: Paste the paper rolls side-by-side in the cardboard piece. Cut off the extra length.

Step 5: Fill paper rolls over the front panel of cardboard. Attach 2 more edges of cardboard frame, leaving one side open to insert photo.

Step 6: Take another piece of cardboard and attach on the back as shown in above fig.

Step 7: Fix the back support.

Step 8: Now the photo frame is ready!

2. Pen pot

Step 1: Prepare paper rolls with glue, bend them into three parts uniformly.

Step 2: Stick together all rolls in center as shown in fig.

Step 3: Bend it in sides and tie it using thin metal wires.

Step 4: If should form a cyllindrical shape while tie up with wire.

Step 5: Make 3 - 4 levels of wire circles

Step 6: Tada!! Pen pot is ready!

You can be much more creative with paper rolls!
